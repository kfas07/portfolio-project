About Me

Ever since I was a child I was always interested in computers and computer programs. Anytime I had to make a powerpoint presentation in school I would always go above and beyond by adding fun transitions, music, and animations. My mom had her own graphic design business and I was fascinated by her creative process. When I came to college I took an intro level computer science class that introduced me to coding. I instantly loved it and knew I wanted to make a career out of it.
