Career

Withing coding there are 2 main focuses. Front-End web development and Back-End web development. Front-End is what you see when you open a website. It is the text, the style, and the content. Back-End is all of the content that you do not see. It is how the website works and the reason it is interactive and funtional. I hope to find a job in the Front-End field. I love typing code and watching a webpage go from a blank file to a beautilfully designed website. Seeing what a website looks like behind the scenes is the most interesting part to me. They look nothing alike and seeing my work come to life is so cool.

Above is a video of a program I coded that works like a magic eightball. Everytime I click "Save" it generates a different answer to your question just like an eight ball would when you shake it. In this case my question was "Will I make an A on my mini independant study" I hope the eight ball answers in my favor!
